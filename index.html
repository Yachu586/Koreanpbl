역사
HALLYU
WAVE
1992
The Birth of K-Pop
Seo Taiji and Boys debut, blending Western hip-hop with Korean music. This moment is widely considered the origin of modern K-Pop as a genre.
1997
First Hallyu Wave
Korean dramas begin spreading across Asia, triggering the first Hallyu (Korean Wave) phenomenon especially in China and Southeast Asia.
2012
Gangnam Style Goes Viral
PSY's "Gangnam Style" becomes the first YouTube video to hit 1 billion views, putting K-Pop on the global map overnight.
2017–2019
BTS Conquers the West
BTS becomes the first K-Pop act to top the Billboard Hot 100, perform at the AMAs, and address the United Nations.
2021
Squid Game Changes Everything
Squid Game becomes Netflix's most-watched series ever, winning major Emmy Awards and proving Korean storytelling could dominate global TV.
2025+
The New Wave
K-Pop's 4th generation pioneers new metaverse and AI concepts. K-dramas continue winning global awards. Hallyu is now a permanent fixture of world pop culture.

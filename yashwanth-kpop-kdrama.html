역사
HALLYU
WAVE
1992
The Birth of K-Pop
Seo Taiji and Boys debut, blending Western hip-hop with Korean music. This moment is widely considered the origin of modern K-Pop as a genre.
1997
First Hallyu Wave
Korean dramas like "What is Love?" and music begin spreading across Asia, triggering the first Hallyu (Korean Wave) phenomenon especially in China and Southeast Asia.
2005
BoA & DBSK Go Global
SM Entertainment artists dominate Japan and begin breaking into Western markets. The "Big 3" agency system (SM, YG, JYP) solidifies its dominance.
2012
Gangnam Style Goes Viral
PSY's "Gangnam Style" becomes the first YouTube video to hit 1 billion views, putting K-Pop on the global map overnight and sparking massive Western curiosity.
2017–2019
BTS Conquers the West
BTS becomes the first K-Pop act to top the Billboard Hot 100, perform at the AMAs, and address the United Nations. K-Pop officially enters mainstream Western culture.
2021
Squid Game Changes Everything
Squid Game becomes Netflix's most-watched series ever, winning major Emmy Awards and proving that Korean storytelling could dominate not just music but global TV.
2025+
The New Wave
K-Pop's 4th generation (aespa, NewJeans, ATEEZ, IVE) pioneers new metaverse and AI concepts. K-dramas continue to win global awards. Hallyu is now a permanent fixture of world pop culture.
